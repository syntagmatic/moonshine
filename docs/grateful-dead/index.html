The Grateful Dead: 2,358 Shows

Six interactive explorations of 30 years of Grateful Dead concerts, built from the gdshowsdb database of every show from 1965 to 1995.

The Grateful Dead played 2,358 shows over 30 years, performing 436 unique songs a total of 39,774 times. No two shows were the same. These explorations use real setlist data to reveal the patterns behind the improvisation: how the repertoire evolved, where the band toured, what grammar governed their setlists, how songs flowed into each other, which songs traveled as companions, and how six distinct musical eras shaped the Dead's identity.

Each piece is a self-contained interactive article. Start anywhere. The data comes from Jef Smith's gdshowsdb, a comprehensive database of every known Grateful Dead performance.
